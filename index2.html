What is this?

A staging area for viewing EAD's Tech Catalog and Tech Stack changes prior to inclusion

How can I use it?

Add changes to Staged pages using the Samples as guidelines. Next the EAGB products committee reviews the staged products. Then changes are made or they are migrated to the Cataliog or Tech Stacks. View our how-to guide

Who is it for?

This is intended for EAD product specialists, developers looking for influence on product look and feel, and the EAGB product governance committee. But, since it's open source, anyone can take advantage of it. If it's useful to you, use it.

Why did we make it?

We have a lot of products at the USPTO. This design sheet should help and resources aim to create a unified experience across all products at the USPTO, both internal and external.

Not using the Product Mapper?

That's fine! Products are welcome to use this mapper, but not all of them do. The samples and formatting guidelines could still be useful.
